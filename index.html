Our Story

Cherry Bean Café is a black woman-owned coffee shop in Chicago that serves up delicious coffee, pastries, and sandwiches. Since our opening in July 2018, our goal has been to provide a space where people can come to relax and enjoy good food and drinks. We also want to give back to our community by providing jobs and opportunities for black people.
